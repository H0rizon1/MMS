About MMS

Building a community of responsible mountaineers

Mission

To promote mountaineering as a sport and advocate for environment consservation through responsible outdoor activities.

Vision

To be the leading mountaineeering organization in the Philippines, fostering a community of passionate and responsible outdoor enthusiasts.

Our Objectives

Organize regular climbs and outdoor activities
Provide quality mountaineering education through BMC
Promote Leave No Trace principles
Build a strong community of mountaineers
Contribute to mountain conservation efforts
MMS Creed

"We are mountaineers. We respect nature, we help fellow climbers, and we leave no trace. We climb not to conquer mountains, but to conquer ourselves."

Our Journey

1994
MMS was founded at the summit of Mt. Makiling by eight passionate mountaineers.
1996
First Basic Mountaineering Course conducted.
2005
Reached 500 active members across Metro Manila.
2010
Launched environmental conservation programs and the Leave No Trace advocacy.
2020
Adapted to virtual training during the pandemic, keeping the community active and connected.
2024
Celebrated 1,000+ successful climbs and the society's 30th anniversary.
